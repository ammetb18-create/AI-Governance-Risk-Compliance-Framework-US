# AI Governance & Risk Compliance Framework for U.S. Financial Systems

This repository presents a structured and applied framework developed to address critical risks associated with artificial intelligence (AI) systems in financial and regulated environments within the United States.

## Objective

To design and implement practical methodologies for:

- AI risk identification and classification  
- Algorithmic bias detection and mitigation  
- Regulatory compliance alignment (ECOA, FCRA, SR 11-7, NIST AI RMF)  
- Cybersecurity risk integration  

## Problem

Artificial intelligence systems are increasingly used in:

- Credit scoring  
- Insurance underwriting  
- Automated decision-making  

These systems introduce risks such as:

- Algorithmic bias  
- Lack of transparency  
- Regulatory non-compliance  
- Cybersecurity vulnerabilities  

## Solution

This framework provides:

- Risk classification models  
- Governance and compliance structures  
- Bias detection methodologies  
- Practical implementation strategies  

## Real-World Application (United States)

This framework is designed to be applied in U.S.-based financial institutions.

Example:

A lending or insurance company can use this framework to:

1. Classify AI risk levels  
2. Detect bias in decision-making systems  
3. Align models with U.S. regulations  
4. Implement continuous monitoring
